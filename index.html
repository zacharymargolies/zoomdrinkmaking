Zoom Drink Making

Ingredients

Must Have - Liquors

  • Whisky
  • Tequila (blanco)
  • Red wine (preferably something dry w/ fruity notes)
  • Gin

Must Have - Other Ingredients

  • Lots of ice
  • An Egg
  • Several Lemons
    • Several ounces of lemon juice
    • Lemon twist
  • Several Limes
    • Several ounces of lime juice
    • Lime wedges
  • A grapefruit or two
    • Grapefruit juice
    • Grapefruit wedge
  • Simple Syrup (easy to make at home)
  • Club Soda
  • Mint leaves

Optional - Ingredients

  • Curacao
  • Maple syrup (preferable to simple syrup in New York Sour recipe)
  • Agave syrup (preferable to simple syrup in La Paloma recipe)

Preparation (before the Zoom)

  • Make lots of ice (or buy some)
  • Make simple syrup if you're not using maple syrup / agave syrup
  • Make several ounces of lemon juice
    • Make lemon twist
  • Make several ounces of lime juice
    • Make lime slice garnishes
  • Put out all shakers and drink making stuff
