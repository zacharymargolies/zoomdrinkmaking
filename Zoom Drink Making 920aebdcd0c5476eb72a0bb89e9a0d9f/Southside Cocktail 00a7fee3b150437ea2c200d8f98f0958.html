Southside Cocktail

Flavor profile: mint

Ingredients

  • 2 oz gin
  • 1 oz simple syrup
  • .75 oz lime wedges
  • Splash of club soda

Instructions

  • Pour .75 oz lime juice into cocktail shaker. Drop lime wedges in.
  • Add 1oz simple syrup, and mint to shaker. Muddle mint to bruise leaves and extra oil.
  • Add gin and ice.
  • Shake.
  • Add splash of soda and pour into a highball glass.
  • Enjoy.
